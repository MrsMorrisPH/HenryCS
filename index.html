About the Pathway

Information and Communication Technology: Software and Systems Development

Build the skills to shape the future! Our pathway introduces students to problem-solving, design thinking, and the foundations of computer science. Explore exciting fields like web development, user experience, networking, data analysis, and software engineering. Along the way, earn college credit through AP courses and dual enrollment, gain industry-recognized certifications, and work on real projects with mentors. From hands-on learning to career-ready experiences, this pathway opens doors to a variety of careers and college success.
